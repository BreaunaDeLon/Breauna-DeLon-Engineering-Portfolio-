Project Overview

This project involved redesigning the front and rear suspension systems for a Formula SAE vehicle with emphasis on packaging constraints, manufacturability, and rule compliance.

Engineering Contributions

  • Suspension geometry development in SolidWorks
  • Packaging optimization for chassis and drivetrain constraints
  • Comparison of rocker-arm vs direct-acting coilover configurations
  • Design validation using hand calculations and FEA
